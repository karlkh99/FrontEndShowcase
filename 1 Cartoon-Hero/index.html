About Reggie

Regina is a 12 year who likes herself enough to never want to grow up. She often escapes to Endless Island where everyone isn't obsessed about being old and stupid and boring.


On endlesss Reggie never has to grow up, and she gets to be the superstrong superhero Twelve who is adored by all of the island's strange inhabitants.
